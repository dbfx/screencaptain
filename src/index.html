To start recording click the button above and select a window or your whole desktop. You will then see a preview and can click the Record button. Once you are done click Stop and choose a path to save the file.



Free modern screen recording

Screen Captain is a free app made by Dave Blakey. It allows you to record your screen or a specific window and outputs webm files, primarily for web use.
